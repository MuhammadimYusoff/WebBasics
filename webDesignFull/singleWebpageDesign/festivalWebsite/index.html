Chicago Jazz Festival

The oldest of Chicago's free lakefront music festivals boasts an incredible diverse lineup, which includes free neighborhood concerts leading up to the main show at Millenium Park each August. The range of artists comprising the Chicago Jazz Festival runs the gamut from jazz legends to influential modern masters and crucial new voices in the genre's continuing evolution
